{{ lucide_icon("info","w-4 h-4",16)|safe }} Meme Coin Warning Signs

  • •Unverified contracts are dangerous: If the smart contract is not verified on Etherscan/BscScan, the team can hide malicious code like mint functions, blacklists, or honeypot mechanics. Never invest in unverified contracts.
  • •Extreme hype with no substance: Meme coins rely on social media hype, not fundamentals. Very high hype + anonymous team + no audit is a classic rug pull setup. Check if the hype is organic or paid.
  • •Unlocked liquidity means exit risk: If liquidity is not locked, the developers can withdraw all funds from the pool at any time. Look for projects that lock liquidity for at least 1-2 years with a reputable locker.
  • •Anonymous teams are a red flag: While not always a scam, anonymous teams are much harder to hold accountable. Legitimate projects usually have doxxed founders or at least a known track record in the space.
