Will inflation destroy my retirement savings?

This is our special market-gap feature. Other calculators tell you that you will have $2 Million in 30 years. But because of inflation, things will cost a lot more in 30 years! Our calculator automatically discounts your final number by the inflation rate (usually 3%). This shows you the True Purchasing Power of your money in today's dollars so you know exactly what your savings are actually worth.

The Magic of Compound Interest

Albert Einstein called compound interest the "Eighth Wonder of the World." When your money earns interest, that interest gets added to your total. Then, your interest starts earning its own interest! Over 20 or 30 years, this causes your monthly contributions to snowball into massive wealth.
