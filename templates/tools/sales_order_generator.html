About the Free Sales Order Generator

Professional SO Maker

A Sales Order (SO) is an internal document generated by a supplier after receiving a Purchase Order from a buyer. It confirms the sale and sets the expectations for fulfillment. Our generator makes this process seamless and professional.

Advanced Math Engine

The WYSIWYG editor allows you to set independent Tax / GST percentages for every single line item. It automatically aggregates total taxes, adds flat-rate shipping, subtracts discounts, and provides a perfect Grand Total.

Branding Optimized

Use the built-in color accent selector to match the document to your brand's specific color palette. Upload your logo to create beautifully branded PDFs that impress your B2B clientele.

Zero Server Logging

Sales orders often contain sensitive client lists and pricing discounts. This tool runs 100% inside your local browser cache. Absolutely zero data is logged, tracked, or sent to any remote server.
