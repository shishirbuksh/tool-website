What is a SIP?

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) lets you invest a small amount of money every month in mutual funds. It is an easy way to save money regularly without worrying about the stock market going up or down.

How Your Money Grows

When you invest, your money earns profit. Then, that profit earns more profit. Over time, your small monthly savings can grow into a huge amount of money. The earlier you start, the more money you make.

Step-Up SIP (Increase Every Year)

As you earn more money each year, you should invest more. The "Step-Up SIP" feature lets you automatically increase your monthly investment every year. This is the fastest way to build your wealth.

Easy to Understand Charts

Our tool shows you exactly how much money you invested and how much profit you made. You can clearly see your money grow year by year with our simple and visual charts.
