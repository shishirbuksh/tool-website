In most countries (like the US and Europe), it is illegal for a commercial truck driver to drive 24 hours straight. A solo driver is usually limited to about 10-11 hours of driving per day. This means a 30-hour drive actually takes 3 full days of transit time!

A team driver setup means there are two drivers in the truck. While one driver sleeps in the back, the other drives. This allows the truck to move almost 22 hours a day, cutting transit times in half. Our calculator has a "Team Driver" option to estimate this!
