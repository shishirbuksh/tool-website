About the Free Receipt Generator

Thermal POS Design

Our receipt generator is specifically designed to perfectly mimic standard 80mm thermal POS (Point of Sale) printer rolls. It utilizes monospaced fonts, jagged edge bottom borders, and grayscale logo filtering for ultimate realism.

WYSIWYG Live Editing

Click directly on the receipt to edit store names, addresses, line items, and tax labels. No clunky side-forms required. The math engines automatically calculate subtotals and totals as you type.

Print & PDF Support

Hitting the Print/PDF button engages specialized CSS @media print queries. This strips the background UI and perfectly scales the receipt width to 80mm for direct thermal printing or clean PDF saving.

Zero Server Tracking

Store data and financial transaction amounts are never uploaded to any server. This entire application runs locally inside your browser, ensuring 100% data privacy for your business.
