Why ATS Optimization is Crucial for Job Portals

When you apply for a job on portals like LinkedIn, Naukri.com, or Indeed, your resume is almost always scanned by an Applicant Tracking System (ATS) before a human recruiter ever sees it. These platforms use advanced algorithms to parse your experience, skills, and education. If your resume uses complex formatting, hidden tables, or lacks industry-standard keywords, the ATS will score you poorly—resulting in instant rejection.
