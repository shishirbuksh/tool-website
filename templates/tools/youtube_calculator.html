Advertisers bid on who sees their ads. Brands in Tier 1 countries (like the US, UK, and Canada) spend more money on advertising than brands in developing countries. Therefore, if your audience is from a Tier 1 country, your RPM (Revenue per 1,000 views) will be significantly higher.

It depends entirely on your niche and audience location. A Finance channel with US viewers might only need 14,000 daily views to make $5,000/month. A Gaming channel with Global viewers might need 111,000 daily views! Use our "Reverse Calculator" feature above to find your exact number.

YouTube Shorts are very fast-paced, so advertisers can't place long, unskippable ads in front of them. Because of this, the RPM is usually between $0.02 and $0.08 per 1,000 views, which is mathematically much lower than standard long-form videos.
