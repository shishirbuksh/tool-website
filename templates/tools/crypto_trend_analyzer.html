How to Use the Tool

  1. Select Your Asset: Choose the cryptocurrency you want to analyze from the dropdown menu.
  2. Check the RSI: Look at the Relative Strength Index. If it's above 70, the asset is 'Overbought'. If below 30, it is 'Oversold'.
  3. Review Moving Averages: Compare the 50-day (Short-Term) moving average against the 200-day (Long-Term) moving average.
  4. Read the Final Signal: The dashboard combines all technicals to give you a clear 'Bullish', 'Bearish', or 'Neutral' market verdict.

How We Calculate the Data

Relative Strength Index (RSI)

Calculated by measuring the magnitude of recent price changes over a 14-day period to evaluate overbought or oversold conditions.

Simple Moving Average (SMA)

We calculate the exact average closing price over the last 50 days and 200 days to establish support and resistance baselines.

Golden Cross Detection

The algorithm checks if the 50-day SMA has crossed above the 200-day SMA, a classic technical signal for a massive bull run.

Death Cross Detection

Conversely, we check if the 50-day SMA has plummeted below the 200-day SMA, warning of a potential long-term bear market.
