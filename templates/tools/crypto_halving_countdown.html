{{ lucide_icon("help-circle","w-4 h-4",16)|safe }} What Is Bitcoin Halving?

Bitcoin halving cuts the block reward in half every 210,000 blocks (roughly 4 years). This limits the supply of new Bitcoin and reduces inflation. Halving events have historically been followed by significant price increases, though past performance does not guarantee future results.
