Frequently Asked Questions

It depends on your website topic. A finance blog that gets readers from Google might only need 16,000 daily views to make $5,000 every month. But a fun news blog getting traffic from Facebook might need 150,000 daily views. You can use our calculator above to find your exact number!

Premium ad networks like Mediavine pay much more money than Google AdSense. Usually, they pay 3 to 5 times more! AdSense pays you only when someone clicks an ad. Premium networks pay you just for showing the ad to a reader, even if they never click it.

People who read tech blogs are very good with computers. Studies show that up to 50% of these readers use AdBlockers. If a reader blocks your ad, you do not make any money from them. Recipe and lifestyle blogs are lucky because their readers rarely block ads!
