How We Calculate the Data

Total Portfolio Valuation

We multiply the quantity of each asset by its real-time market price, then sum them together for your total Net Asset Value (NAV).

Percentage Allocation Weighting

Calculated by dividing the fiat value of an individual asset by the Total Portfolio Valuation, then multiplying by 100.

Large-Cap Dominance Ratio

We sum the percentages of Top 10 assets (like BTC and ETH) to generate a 'stability score' versus micro-cap altcoins.

Rebalancing Deltas

If you input target percentages, the tool subtracts your current allocation from the target to show exactly how much fiat you need to buy or sell.
