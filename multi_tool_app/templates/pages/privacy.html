Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At StoryBrain AI, we prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy documents what information we collect and how we use it.

Data Collection and Usage

1. Client-Side Tools: Most of our tools (e.g., Password Generator, Age Calculator, Percentage Calculator) run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. We do not send this data to our servers, nor do we save it.

2. Server-Side Tools: Tools that require server processing (e.g., QR Code Generator and Base64 conversion) transmit your input securely to our backend via HTTPS. This data is processed in memory and strictly discarded immediately after the result is returned. No logs are kept of user inputs.

Cookies and Local Storage

We use standard modern web browser features like localStorage purely to enhance user experience—such as remembering your Dark/Light theme preference. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

Contact

If you have questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us on our Contact Page.
